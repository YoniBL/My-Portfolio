VueICL: Enhancing Video Understanding with Entity-Aware AI

As part of a university workshop, I collaborated with two colleagues and a researcher from Bar-Ilan University to develop an advanced AI model for entity-aware video question answering. Our approach integrates face recognition with cutting-edge multimodal AI models to enable precise identification and reasoning about individuals in videos. The project was documented following the ICML standard, one of the fastest-growing AI conferences in the world.
